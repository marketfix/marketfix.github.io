How to write a killer landing page?

What is a landing page?
It's a website with the sole purpose of perceiving information about your product or service as quickly as possible. Your landing page is the first impression your potential clients and customers will get of your business. It needs to have an impact and concisely inform about your offerings. A solid landing page will improve your conversion rate and help you achieve your desired results. A landing page should be single-product oriented and not be confused with a brand website.

Focus on your heading
The heading should emphasize exactly what sets you apart. Make it bold and enticing to draw people in. A landing page should be short and to the point, providing only the information that is relevant to what the user is looking for. Anything else that is included on the page risks distracting the user and causing them to leave without taking action. Therefore, it is important to only include information that is relevant and necessary on a landing page.

Use your subheading
The subheading is a compliment to the heading you’ve written. It is a way to clarify what you offer in a succinct way. Subheadings can help to organize your thoughts and make your writing more understandable. They can also be used to highlight important information or to make your writing more visually appealing. Give your website visitor a reason to want to learn more about what you do.

Have a strong Call to Action
Tailor the Call to Action for your audience. Another element that leads to a high conversion, the CTA acts as a way to guide visitors to your final point of sale. Choose a phrase that would speak to your potential client or consumer rather than a generic “Learn More”. Make sure the website is designed in a way where the visitor feels in control and has multiple patch to take, but eventually gets redirected to the final point of sale regardless.

Visual look matters
To make your landing page visually appealing, make sure the design is up to date and usable from any smart device. A visually appealing design will help you stand out from the competition and give visitors the impression that you’re a professional, reliable business. A responsive design is essential, as more and more people are using mobile devices to browse the web. Make sure your landing page looks great on all screen sizes by using a responsive design template.
